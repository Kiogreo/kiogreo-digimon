## Specialization Tendency
**Primary**: {specialization}
**Tendency Strength**: {60-95}%

## Description

## Field Guidelines

### digitama_id
- Format: `{timestamp}_{random_3digit}`
- Example: `20260114_847`
- Must be unique

### name
- User-provided or auto-generated
- Should be memorable and fitting
- Examples: "FireDragon", "WisdomEgg", "SparkBuddy"

### potential_level
- Scale: 1-10
- 1-3: Basic potential (standard growth)
- 4-6: Good potential (above-average)
- 7-9: High potential (accelerated)
- 10: Exceptional (rare)

### traits
- Select 3-5 from trait list
- Should complement specialization
- Examples: Determined, Playful, Clever, Bold, Curious

### specialization
- One of 9 specializations:
  * engineering, scientist, arts, politics, business
  * data-analysis, creative-writing, support, research

### tendency_strength
- 60-75%: Moderate tendency (flexible development)
- 76-90%: Strong tendency (clear direction)
- 91-95%: Very strong tendency (highly specialized)

### description
- 2-3 sentences
- Evoke the Digitama's essence
- Make it feel special and unique
- Examples:
  * "This egg pulses with creative energy, its surface shimmering with colors that shift like a living canvas. There's an artistic soul waiting to emerge."
  * "Steady and methodical, this Digitama emanates an aura of logical precision. It seems to analyze everything around it, even before hatching."
